About Universal Urns

Universal Urns proudly offers Made-in-America aluminum urns designed for columbariums, niche walls, and direct burial. Every urn is crafted using precision laser technology and finished with durable powder coating to ensure long-lasting beauty and respect.

We are committed to honoring every life with dignity, offering products that serve both aesthetic and functional needs for memorial services, cemeteries, and families alike.
