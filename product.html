Our Universal urnS

The round and rectangle urns are precisely sized to fit in niche walls and columbariums. Our product is made of heavy-duty 16-gauge aluminum, and powder-coated to perfection. All urns are cut with precision laser lathes. The urns are crush resistant. The urns are designed to hold between 200 and 225 cubic inches of ashes.Engineer-tested to hold up to 15,000 psi pressure, which certifies them to be buried directly in the ground.

Styles: Rectangle, Cylinder

Colors: Hammered Gold, Hammered Brown (not customizable)

Finish: Hammered texture for lasting durability
